# Thesis Reader Guide

Read in this order:

1. `docs/thesis_scope.md`
2. `README.md`
3. `docs/metric_families.md`
4. `results/thesis_final/final_report.md`
5. `results/thesis_final/provider_comparison.csv`
6. `results/thesis_final/quality_table.csv`
7. `results/thesis_final/performance_table.csv`
8. `results/thesis_final/noise_robustness_table.csv`
9. `results/thesis_final/cost_deployment_table.csv`
10. `reports/datasets/dataset_asset_validation.md`
11. `reports/thesis_test/thesis_evidence_validation.md`

Baseline thesis evidence:

- `results/thesis_final/`
- `artifacts/benchmark_runs/thesis_*`
- `reports/datasets/`
- `reports/thesis_test/`

Extended multi-dataset evidence, after the extended benchmark is generated:

- `results/thesis_extended/`
- `results/runs_ext/`
- `artifacts/benchmark_runs/thesis_ext_*`
- `datasets/registry/datasets_extended.json`

Historical, generated and development artifacts are not part of final thesis interpretation. Legacy compatibility outputs are archived under `results/archive_legacy/`.
